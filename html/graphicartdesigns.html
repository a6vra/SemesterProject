Graphic Art Designs

This page showcases my currently finished graphic art projects. This includes a logo for a band as well as art of a brand mascot. Looking towards the future, I am working on more logos and learning about commercial art and art for media (such as the cover of a book).

Github Octocat Mascot

This project is from the NJIT Hackathon, November 2021. There was a contest held by Github where participants were encouraged to draw art of their mascot, the Octocat. This art piece ended up winning that competition, and overall the process of drawing a mascot for a brand was fun to make.

Metal Band Logo

This project was done as a commission for a client that was starting up a band and needed a logo. This was the first time I ever tried a logo, and it was a good learning experience to design one. I was given a handful of ideas and concepts that my client wanted to be reflected in the piece. From that point on I had to rely on those ideas and references (popular metal band logos) to draw the finished product.
